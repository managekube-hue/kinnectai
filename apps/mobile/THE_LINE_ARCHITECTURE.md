## Data Flow: Loading Feed

```
User Opens App
     ?
     ??? LineCubit.loadFeed()
     ?        ?
     ?        ??? FeedService.getLine()
     ?        ?        ?
     ?        ?        ??? Check SharedPreferences cache
     ?        ?        ?        ?
     ?        ?        ?        ??? Cache Hit? ? Return cached feed
     ?        ?        ?        ?
     ?        ?        ?        ??? Cache Miss? ? Call LineApiClient
     ?        ?        ?                 ?
     ?        ?        ?                 ??? NetworkErrorHandler.executeWithRetry()
     ?        ?        ?                 ?        ?
     ?        ?        ?                 ?        ??? Attempt 1: GET /api/v1/line/feed
     ?        ?        ?                 ?        ?        ?
     ?        ?        ?                 ?        ?        ??? Success? ? Return feed
     ?        ?        ?                 ?        ?        ?
     ?        ?        ?                 ?        ?        ??? Failed? ? Retry (2x more)
     ?        ?        ?                 ?        ?
     ?        ?        ?                 ?        ??? Parse response ? List<Memory>
     ?        ?        ?                 ?
     ?        ?        ?                 ??? Cache result in SharedPreferences
     ?        ?        ?
     ?        ?        ??? Return List<Memory>
     ?        ?
     ?        ??? emit(LineLoaded(memories))
     ?        ?
     ?        ??? VideoCache.preloadBatch(memories, 0)
     ?                 ?
     ?                 ??? Preload videos 0, 1, 2
     ?
     ??? UI renders PageView with videos
              ?
              ??? EnhancedLineVideoPlayer shows video 0
```

## Error Handling Flow

```
API Call ? NetworkErrorHandler.executeWithRetry()
    ?
    ??? Attempt 1
    ?   ??? Success? ? Return result
    ?   ??? Failed?
    ?       ??? isRetryable(error)?
    ?           ??? Yes ? Wait 2s ? Attempt 2
    ?           ?   ??? Failed? ? Wait 4s ? Attempt 3
    ?           ?       ??? Failed? ? Return error
    ?           ?
    ?           ??? No ? Return error immediately
    ?
    ??? Result<T>
        ??? Success ? result.data
        ??? Failure ? result.error
            ??? getUserMessage(error)
                ??? "No internet connection. Please check your network."
```
